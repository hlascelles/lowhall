Location

Low Hall lies in the Duddon Valley in the south central part of the Lake District National Park.

The nearest houses are half a mile away in the hamlet of Hall Dunnerdale. A bit further on, about 20 minutes walk from the house, is the hamlet of Seathwaite which has a church and the very nice Newfield Inn pub. Broughton (20 mins by car) has several small shops, an off-licence and good pubs. There are supermarkets further afield for groceries, and Tesco also delivers to Low Hall.

Travel time from London to Low Hall (275 miles) is about five hours.
