The living room

Low Hall can accommodate six people in the main house and four more in the converted barn alongside.

The ground floor consists of a flag-stoned hall with a large dining table, an eat-in kitchen with Aga, and an L-shaped drawing room and study with gas log fire. The house has a telephone, Freeview television, DVD player, hifi, wireless broadband and a piano.

Upstairs are three bedrooms. The master bedroom has a four poster bed and connected bathroom. The second bedroom has a double bed. The third has a single bed and full size bunks, with shared bathroom and separate loo.

Alongside the house are old farm buildings with a large bracken loft reached up outside stone steps which sleeps five: a double bed, and three singles in dormitory accommodation. The loft bedroom has its own bathroom and loo, and is electrically heated.

Low Hall has its own garden and a wildflower meadow with an open fire pit.
